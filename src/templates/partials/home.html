Excursions in Lisbon

Our Excursions in Lisbon are full of varieties and different amazing routes which will make you see Lisbon with the eyes of the local people. Our interpreter-guides are local and all qualified professionals, friendly persons and they love working with Lisbon visitors. Our favorite monument in Lisbon is Monsteiro dos Jeronimos. It is outstanding architectural monument which attracts a lot of tourists every year. It took nearly a hundred years to build that great monument and it has pure Portuguese architectural style which is called Manuelinho. Also that monument is a kind of national Renaissance and it has got elements of Gothic and Moorish styles. That Monestary was a shelter for a long time for Jerome monks who served very well to the king Manuel I. It has been many years when the Monestary was dissolved and became a national property of Portugal. Now it is a Archaeological Museum which you can visit with our help.
